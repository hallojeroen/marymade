Hi, I'm Mary. A busy mom, professional chef and certified nutritionist. I'm orginally from Canada and have worked all over Europe. Fast forward 10 years, now settled down in Utrecht with a Dutchie and have 3 beautifull daughters. It's been a rollercoaster to say the least. Finding out that I was expecting twins (already having a 1,5 year old toddler) was the greatest gift but also the biggest challenge of my life. I struggled to find the time and energy to prepare wholsome meals for myself and my family. While I was pregnant I had no desire to cook. As much as I knew I needed to nourish myself and my baby(s). Every smell in the kitchen made me nausious. I realized that I could help other mamas (and their families) in this busy period by starting a healthy (pre/post) pregnancy meal service. By combining my two passions: cooking and nutrition, MamaMeals was born.
